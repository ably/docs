Push notifications pop up on a mobile device or computer from an app or website, even when the app is not open. They deliver information, such as app updates, social media alerts, or promotional offers, directly to the user's screen. Ably can send Push notifications to devices using Apple's Push Notification service and Google's Firebase Cloud Messaging service. Unlike Ably's usual channel-based messaging, Push notifications don't always require the device to stay connected to Ably. Instead, the operating system iOS or Android handles the connection. Optimized connection handling establishes and maintains connections as needed, minimizing the time the device spends actively communicating with external services — saving battery usage.

h3. Configure

Push notification "configuration":/docs/setup#channel-publishing sets up the infrastructure for the device's operating system and the Ably "platform":https://ably.com/push-notifications. This setup includes communication with Push services and configuring credentials in the Ably "dashboard":https://ably.com/accounts. The final step involves testing the notification flow to confirm successful registration and the receipt of notifications.

h3. Activate

Activate devices "directly":/docs/push/setup#device-activation from a device or from your "server-side:":/docs/push/#server-based-activation

* The "direct":/docs/push/setup#device-activation method activates the device for push notifications with the underlying operating system or platform and obtains a unique identifier for the device as a push recipient.

* The "server-side":/docs/push/#server-based-activation method delegates the activation process to your server, involving server-side configuration. This method is more commonly used to minimize the capabilities assigned to an untrusted device

h3. Subscribe

Subscribe devices to receive push notifications using "deviceID":/docs/push/#subscribe-device-id or "clientID:":/docs/push/#subscribe-client-id

* The "deviceID":/docs/push/#subscribe-device-id method subscribes a device directly to a channel using its unique deviceID, assigned by Ably, for push notifications upon device activation. The direct method links the subscription specifically to the device.

* The "clientID":/docs/push/#subscribe-client-id method subscribes all devices tied to a particular clientID to a channel in one action. This approach is useful for subscribing multiple devices simultaneously - streamlining subscription management.

h3. Publish

Publish push notifications "directly":#channel-pub to a device or through "Channels:":#channel-pub

* "Direct":/docs/publish#direct-publishing publishing allows for the delivery of Push notifications straight to individual devices. It enables targeting based on unique deviceIDs, clientIDs associated with one or more devices, or recipient details specific to the push notification service.

* "Channel-based":docs/publish#channel-publishing publishing operates similarly to Ably's "Pub/Sub":/docs/channels/messages messaging model, broadcasting notifications to all channel subscribers. This method leverages Ably "Channels":/docs/Channels to distribute Push notifications to all devices subscribed to those Channels.
