Manage your Ably account settings, including authentication, billing, and ownership transfer options.

h3. Settings

Use the account ID for managing integrations or when contacting support.

- Account ID: := The unique identifier associated with your account.

h3. Authentication settings

Prompt users associated with your account to set up two-factor authentication ("2FA":/account/2fa) to do so the next time they try to access the account.

- Require two-factor authentication for all account users: := Select to prompt users.

h3. Billing and packages

Update or modify your "billing":/pricing/billing and package details.

h3. Change account owner

As the current account owner, you are the only one who can access this page and modify billing or package information. To transfer ownership of this account to another person, contact "Ably support.":https://ably.com/support

h3. Cancel your account

Canceling your account will permanently delete all data and settings, and you won't be charged again (except for any outstanding usage fees). If you are sure, contact "Ably support":https://ably.com/support to cancel your account immediately.
