Ably's platform architecture is built to deliver dependable realtime experiences at global scale. As the definitive realtime experience platform, we serve billions of devices monthly, powering critical applications for businesses like HubSpot, NASCAR, and Webflow with unmatched reliability and performance.

h3. At a Glance 

Our multi-region infrastructure forms the foundation of our platform, allowing us to maintain exceptional performance while serving massive scale. This globally distributed architecture doesn't just provide redundancy - it ensures that even during regional failures or network partitions, your realtime applications continue to function seamlessly.

We characterise our system across "4 pillars of dependability":https://ably.com/four-pillars-of-dependability :

* **Performance**: We focus on predictability of latencies to provide certainty in uncertain operating conditions.
** <50ms global median latency
** 19M+ concurrent connections
* **Reliability**: Ably is designed to preserve continuity of service we ensure sufficient redundancy at a regional and global level.
** 5+ years since last outage
** 99.999999% message survivability
* **Scale**: Our service is elastic, highly-available and built to handle extreme scale and large numbers of concurrent connections.
** 1B+ connected devices per month
** 2T+ API operations per month
* **Network**: We operate a truly distributed global edge network to delivers a globally consistent experience to users.
** 635 points of presence
** 11 globally distributed regions
