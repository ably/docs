h2. Manage your billing

Managing your package settings in Ably is essential for scaling your services and ensuring accurate billing.

h3. Understand your current package

The following table explains the Ably package types:

|_. Package type |_. Description |
| Free | Ably's free package enables developers quickly build and test realtime applications by providing essential messaging and connection capabilities at no cost, ideal for proof-of-concept projects. |
| Pay-as-you-go | Ably's pay-as-you-go package enables developers to scale realtime applications by paying for usage, offering higher limits on messages, connections, and channels with additional options for burst capacity and better support. |
| Committed use | Ably's committed Use plan provides customized, discounted pricing for businesses with predictable usage, offering higher limits, enhanced burst capacity, and 24/7 support tailored to their realtime application needs. |

h3. Update your billing details

The following table explains the billing information section:

|_. Billing information |_. Description |
| Company information | Enter your company name, optional billing email address, and physical address. |
| Telephone number | Provide an international contact number for any potential account issues. |
| Timezone | Set your timezone for accurate billing and support. |

h3. Invoices and payment methods

Once your account is upgraded, payment methods and invoices will appear in the *invoices* section.

h3. Transfer account ownership

As the current account owner, you are the only person authorized to access billing pages and change your package or billing information.
To transfer ownership, "contact":https://ably.com/contact Ably for assistance.
