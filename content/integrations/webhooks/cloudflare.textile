"Cloudflare Workers":https://workers.cloudflare.com enable Cloudflare's Edge Network to distribute your JavaScript-based functions.

To trigger a webhook in response to Ably channel events:

* Go to your Ably "dashboard":https://ably.com/dashboard/any" and select your app.
* Navigate to the *Integrations* tab.
* Click *New Integration Rule*.
* In the *Select your rule type* section, choose *webhook*.
* Under *Select service integration*, select *webhook*.
* Fill in the required fields:
** *URL* - The endpoint where Ably will POST a summary of events.
* (Optional) Add custom *Headers* as key:value pairs for additional information.
** Click *Another header* to add more.
* Choose the *Request Mode*:
** "*Single Request*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#single - Sends each event separately.
** "*Batch Request*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#batched - Sends events in batches every 1 second, with a max number of messages per envelope.
* In the "*Source*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#sources dropdown, select which type of events will trigger the rule:
** Message
** Presence
** Channel Lifecycle
** Channel Occupancy
* (Optional) Add a "*Channel Filter*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#channel-filter using a regular expression to target specific channels. Leave blank to apply to all.
* Choose an "*Encoding*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#encoding format:
** JSON
** MsgPack
* Decide if messages should be "*Enveloped:*":/docs/integrations/webhooks#enveloped
** Enabled by default. If unchecked, the raw payload is sent without Ably metadata.
* Click *Create* to finish setting up the rule.
