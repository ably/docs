Access and manage your Ably account overview, basic information, and account-level details through the dashboard.

The "Your account" section provides an overview of your Ably account and serves as the main entry point for account-level management.

## Accessing your account overview

To view your account information:

1. Click Account in the top navigation bar
2. Select Your account from the dropdown menu
3. You'll see your account overview page

## Account information displayed

The account overview shows:

### Account identification

- Your account name and identifier
- Account creation date and status
- Primary account contact information

### Account status

- Current account plan and subscription status
- Account verification status
- Any pending actions or notifications

### Quick access

The account overview provides quick links to:

- Account settings and configuration
- Billing and subscription management
- Usage monitoring and statistics
- Team and user management

## Common tasks from account overview

### Managing account details

From the account overview you can:

- Update basic account information
- Modify contact details
- Change account preferences
- Access security settings

### Monitoring account health

Use the overview to:

- Check account status and any alerts
- Review recent account activity
- Monitor overall usage trends
- Access support when needed

### Navigation to other sections

The account overview provides easy navigation to:

- [Account settings](/docs/platform/account/account-settings) for detailed configuration
- [Billing management](/docs/platform/account/billing) for subscription and payment details
- [Usage monitoring](/docs/platform/account/usage) for consumption tracking
- [User management](/docs/platform/account/users) for team administration

## Account overview best practices

### Regular monitoring

- Check your account overview regularly for important notifications
- Monitor account status to ensure services remain active
- Review any pending actions that may require attention

### Security awareness

- Verify account information remains current and accurate
- Monitor for any unexpected changes or activity
- Ensure contact information is up to date for security notifications

### Efficient navigation

- Use the account overview as your starting point for account management
- Bookmark frequently accessed sections for quick navigation
- Familiarize yourself with the account dropdown menu structure

The account overview serves as your central hub for understanding and managing your Ably account at a high level, providing both essential information and quick access to detailed management features.
